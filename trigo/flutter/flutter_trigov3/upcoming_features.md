🚦 Dispatcher App

Identified Problems:
	•	Passenger has no cellphone? (Contact and communication issues)
	•	How to earn from passengers paying by cash? (Revenue collection and tracking issues)

Core Features (Solutions):
	•	Ride Monitoring
	•	Driver Assignment
	•	Adjustable Max Passenger Configuration (depends on TODA)
	•	Driver Suspension/Unsuspension: Triggers notifications to Driver, Operator, Admin, and App Monitoring.
	•	Premium Subscription: Enables reporting a driver; multiple reports can lead to driver suspension and grants priority service.

Additional Features:
	•	Ticketing System (replacing traditional dispatcher ticketing; integrated payment gateway)
	•	Real-Time Ride Tracking
	•	Payment Gateway Integration
	•	Analytics Dashboard
	•	Push Notifications
	•	Driver Rating System
	•	Popular Places Insights: Identifies most visited locations for strategic insights and operations planning.
