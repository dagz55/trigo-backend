# TriGo App Workflow

## 1. User Registration and Login

1.1. New users can sign up as either a passenger or a driver.
1.2. Users provide necessary information (name, email, phone number, etc.).
1.3. Drivers must additionally provide vehicle information and necessary documents.
1.4. Users can log in using email/password or social login (Google, Facebook).

## 2. Passenger Workflow

2.1. Book a Ride
   - Enter destination
   - View estimated fare and available drivers
   - Confirm booking

2.2. During the Ride
   - Track driver's location in real-time
   - Communicate with the driver through in-app messaging
   - Option to share ride status with contacts

2.3. After the Ride
   - Rate the driver and provide feedback
   - View ride history and receipts

## 3. Driver Workflow

3.1. Accept Rides
   - Receive ride requests
   - View passenger location and destination
   - Accept or decline rides

3.2. During the Ride
   - Use in-app navigation to reach passenger and destination
   - Communicate with the passenger through in-app messaging

3.3. After the Ride
   - Mark ride as complete
   - Rate the passenger
   - View earnings and ride history

## 4. Payment Process

4.1. Passengers can pay through the app using various methods (cash, credit card, e-wallet)
4.2. Drivers receive payments periodically after TriGo deducts its commission

## 5. Support and Safety

5.1. In-app support for both passengers and drivers
5.2. Emergency button for immediate assistance
5.3. Regular safety checks and driver verification

## 6. Community Features

6.1. Community ratings and reviews
6.2. Local route suggestions and popular destinations
6.3. Community events and driver meetups

## 7. Analytics and Optimization

7.1. Route optimization based on traffic and historical data
7.2. Demand prediction for drivers
7.3. Personalized recommendations for passengers

This workflow outlines the main features and user journeys in the TriGo app. It can be expanded or modified based on specific requirements and feature additions.
